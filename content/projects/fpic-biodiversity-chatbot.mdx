## Overview

A production-oriented chatbot that answers biodiversity questions by retrieving evidence from curated datasets and generating grounded responses.

## Highlights

- Retrieval-first design (RAG) with citations.
- Observability hooks for prompt + retrieval debugging.
- Optimized chunking + hybrid retrieval strategies.
